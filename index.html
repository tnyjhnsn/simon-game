Inspiration

The sounds on my Simon soundwall are based on the the famous five-notes sequence from Close Encounters of the Third Kind, a 1977 American science fiction film.
Stunning galaxy pictures are provided by The Hubble Space Telescope, launched in 1990 into a low-Earth orbit 569 km above the ground.
Quotes used for the difficulty levels are inspired by movies and TV shows: Lost in Space, Apollo 13 Movie, Star Trek, and Star Wars.
Did you know that Chromesthesia (or sound-to-color synesthesia) is a neurological phenomenon in which heard sounds automatically and involuntarily evoke an experience of color?
